The Toy Tracker App will help you keep track of toys that you want, or already have. It will also help you find new Toys you didn't even know were out there! Go to the Dashboard page to save information about you, and then start searching. Have fun!

1. Search

Search for the toys you want. Go to the Search page and type in the name of a character, show, movie or anything else you like.

2. Wishlist

If you like a Toy and don't currently have it, click "Add to Wishlist". This toy will be saved for your to keep track of until you decide to buy it.

3. My Toys

If you already have a Toy in the list, click "Have it". This will help you keep track of the toys you already own, under the My Toys section.
